STRATEGY ANALYSIS:

This "GLASS CANNON TANK" build focuses entirely on:
- MAXIMUM ATTACK: Can output devastating damage with Gates
- MAXIMUM ENDURANCE: Takes minimal damage from Arlong
- ZERO HP: Must avoid ALL damage or die instantly

Key Advantages:
✓ Gates amplify already high ATK (469 × 2.2 = 1031.8 with G2)
✓ High END reduces incoming damage to ~5-10 per hit
✓ Can sustain G2 for long duration with EP pool
✓ All skills unlocked for maximum tactical options

Critical Weakness:
⚠️ ONE hit that gets through = instant death
⚠️ MUST use stagger mechanics perfectly
⚠️ Cannot afford ANY mistakes

Expected Performance vs Arlong:
- Offense: 10/10 (Massive damage output)
- Defense: 9/10 (High END reduces damage taken)
- Survivability: 1/10 (50 HP = one mistake = death)
- Sustainability: 8/10 (Good EP economy)

VIABILITY RATING: ★★★★☆ (Requires perfect execution)

This build demonstrates that HP can be completely neglected IF:
1. You maximize END to reduce damage taken
2. You use stagger effects to skip enemy turns
3. You end the battle quickly with high ATK
